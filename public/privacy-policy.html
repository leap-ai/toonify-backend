Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Toonify ("we," "our," or "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Toonify mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information about you:

  • Personal information: Email address, password (hashed and securely stored), and authentication tokens (for Google and Apple sign-in).
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (if uploaded by the user).
  • Uploaded Images: Images you upload for processing.
  • Generated Images: AI-generated content.
  • Subscription Information: Your Toonify Pro subscription status (e.g., active/inactive, plan type, expiry date) based on information provided by the Apple App Store.
  • Payment Information: We use third-party services (Apple App Store In-App Purchases) to process payments for subscriptions. We do not directly collect or store your credit card details or other financial information. We may receive transaction identifiers or subscription receipt data from the App Store to validate and manage your subscription status.
  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect device identifiers, operating system information, and usage statistics (e.g., features used, variants selected) for analytics and improving the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Process your uploaded images through our third-party service providers (e.g., Fal.AI, Replicate).
  • Display your original and generated images within the app.
  • Manage your subscription status and provide access to Toonify Pro features.
  • Improve, maintain, and secure our services.
  • Respond to your support requests and communications.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Image Processing: Images uploaded are processed using third-party AI services (e.g., Fal.AI, Replicate). Uploaded images may be subject to the policies of these providers.
  • Authentication Services: Google Sign-In and Sign In with Apple for user authentication.
  • Payment Processing: Subscription purchases are managed through Apple App Store In-App Purchase services.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Uploaded and generated images may be stored securely on third-party servers (e.g., Fal.AI, Replicate) as part of the image generation process or temporarily cached for display within the app. We aim to minimize long-term storage of user images where possible.
  • Personal data (e.g., email address, subscription status) is securely stored using industry-standard encryption and security practices.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Request transfer of your personal data
